Our Story

Learn about how we started and what drives us forward

From Passion to Purpose

FitBook was founded in 2020 with a simple mission: to make fitness accessible, enjoyable, and effective for everyone. What started as a small studio with a handful of classes has grown into a thriving fitness community.

Our founder, Alex Thompson, a former professional athlete, recognized the need for a fitness space that catered to all levels of experience and provided personalized attention in a group setting. This vision has shaped our approach to fitness and wellness.

Today, we're proud to offer a wide range of classes taught by expert instructors who are passionate about helping our members achieve their fitness goals.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Passion

We're deeply passionate about fitness and wellness. Our enthusiasm drives us to provide exceptional experiences that inspire our members to embrace a healthy lifestyle.

Community

We foster a supportive, inclusive community where everyone feels welcome, motivated, and connected. Together, we celebrate achievements and overcome challenges.

Excellence

We strive for excellence in everything we do, from the quality of our instruction to the cleanliness of our facilities. We're committed to continuous improvement.

Innovation

We embrace new approaches to fitness, staying current with industry trends and research to provide our members with cutting-edge workouts and training methods.

Balance

We believe in a balanced approach to fitness that incorporates physical activity, proper nutrition, mental wellness, and adequate recovery for sustainable results.

Support

We provide guidance, motivation, and support throughout our members' fitness journeys, helping them overcome obstacles and achieve their personal goals.

Our Journey

Key milestones in our growth and development

2020

The Beginning

FitBook was founded with just three instructors and a single studio space, offering yoga and HIIT classes.

2021

Expansion

We expanded our class offerings to include Pilates, Spinning, and Strength Training, and welcomed five new instructors to our team.

2022

Online Booking Launch

We launched our online booking platform, making it easier for members to reserve spots in their favorite classes.

2023

Community Milestone

We celebrated reaching 1,000 active members and introduced specialized workshops and fitness challenges.

2024

New Location

We opened our second location to accommodate growing demand and introduced virtual training options.

2025

Today

We continue to grow our community, enhance our services, and help our members achieve their fitness goals.
